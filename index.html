The (Jess) West Cinematic Universe

The top 10 most popular movies with 'West' in the title, according to IMDB

Once Upon a Time in the West

1968 Dir. Sergio Leone

As the railroad builders advance unstoppably through the Arizona desert on their way to the sea, Jill arrives in the small town of Flagstone with the intention of starting a new life.

North by Northwest

1959 Dir. Alfred Hitchcock

Advertising man Roger Thornhill is mistaken for a spy, triggering a deadly cross-country chase.

West Side Story

1961 Dir. Jerome Robbins, Robert Wise

In the slums of the upper West Side of Manhattan, tensions are high as a gang of Polish-Americans compete against a gang of recently immigrated Puerto Ricans, but this doesn’t stop two romantics from each gang falling in love.

Wild Wild West

1999 Dir. Barry Sonnenfeld

Legless Southern inventor Dr. Arliss Loveless plans to rekindle the Civil War by assassinating President U.S. Grant. Only two men can stop him: gunfighter James West and master-of-disguise and inventor Artemus Gordon. The two must team up to thwart Loveless’ plans.

A Million Ways to Die in the West

2014 Dir. Seth MacFarlane

As a cowardly farmer begins to fall for the mysterious new woman in town, he must put his new-found courage to the test when her husband, a notorious gun-slinger, announces his arrival.

How the West Was Won

1962 Dir. John Ford, Henry Hathaway, George Marshall

The epic tale of the development of the American West from the 1830s through the Civil War to the end of the century, as seen through the eyes of one pioneer family.

Ingrid Goes West

2017 Dir. Matt Spicer

Ingrid becomes obsessed with a social network star named Taylor Sloane who seemingly has a perfect life. But when Ingrid decides to drop everything and move west to be Taylor’s friend, her behaviour turns unsettling and dangerous.

Red Rock West

1993 Dir. John Dahl

When a promised job for Texan Michael fails to materialize in Wyoming, Mike is mistaken by Wayne to be the hitman he hired to kill his unfaithful wife, Suzanne. Mike takes full advantage of the situation, collects the money, and runs. During his getaway, things go wrong, and soon get worse when he runs into the real hitman, Lyle.

Escort West

1959 Dir. Francis D. Lyon

Seeking a new place to call home, former Confederate soldier Ben Lassiter (Victor Mature) and his daughter meet Beth (Elaine Stewart), whose fiancé is a Union soldier. Lassiter falls for Beth, and when Indians attack, they head to a cavalry camp where Lassiter must battle the Indians as well as Beth’s fiancé.

An American Tail: Fievel Goes West

1991 Dir. Phil Nibbelink, Simon Wells

Some time after the Mousekewitz’s have settled in America, they find that they are still having problems with the threat of cats. That makes them eager to try another home out in the west, where they are promised that mice and cats live in peace. Unfortunately, the one making this claim is an oily con artist named Cat R. Waul who is intent on his own sinister plan.
